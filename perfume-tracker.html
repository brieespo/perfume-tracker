How To Use

One list, five statuses

Every perfume lives on one list and moves through statuses: To Try → Sampling → Owned → Finished or Sold. Use the buttons on each card to change status with one tap — no re-entering data, ever. Finished means you used it all up because you loved it; Sold means it wasn't for you. While sampling, tap 💜 FS-worthy to flag a sample you'd buy a full size of.

Ratings

Tap the stars in the add/edit form — left half of a star for a half step. Ratings are always stored as real numbers (0.5–5), so nothing can ever turn into a date again.

Notes

Paste the notes text straight from the perfume's listing — it's kept exactly as you pasted it, and the app also parses it into structured notes (shown as chips under the paste box). Anything it doesn't recognize appears as an amber chip: tap it to assign it to an existing note, create a new one, or discard it. The app remembers your answer, so the same phrasing never asks twice.

Review queue

The 📝 Review notes button lists perfumes whose pasted notes haven't been confirmed yet (like everything from the spreadsheet import). Each one shows what the parser found — usually you just glance and tap ✓ Looks right. Perfumes only count in the upcoming charts once reviewed, but nothing else waits on it.

Import & export

  • Import from CSV loads the spreadsheet data that ships with the app (one-time).
  • Export Data downloads your whole collection as JSON for safekeeping.
