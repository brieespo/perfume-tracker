How To Use

One list, five statuses

Every perfume lives on one list and moves through statuses: To Try → Sample → Owned → Finished or Sold. Use the buttons on each card to change status with one tap — no re-entering data, ever. Finished means you used it all up because you loved it; Sold means it wasn't for you.

Ratings

Tap the stars in the add/edit form — left half of a star for a half step. Ratings are always stored as real numbers (0.5–5), so nothing can ever turn into a date again.

Notes

Paste the notes text straight from the perfume's listing. It's kept exactly as you pasted it. A future update will parse notes into a structured vocabulary and chart what you love.

Import & export

  • Import from CSV loads the spreadsheet data that ships with the app (one-time).
  • Export Data downloads your whole collection as JSON for safekeeping.
